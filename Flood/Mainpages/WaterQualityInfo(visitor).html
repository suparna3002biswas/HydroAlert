Why is Water Quality Testing Needed?

Water quality testing is essential to ensure that water is safe for consumption and free from harmful contaminants.

Common Water Impurities

Some common impurities found in water include:

  • Bacteria and viruses
  • Heavy metals (e.g., lead, mercury)
  • Pesticides and herbicides
  • Chlorine and other chemicals
