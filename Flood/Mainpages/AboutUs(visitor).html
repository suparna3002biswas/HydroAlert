About HydroAlert

Our Mission

Water is one of the most important aspects of life. But at times, water can also be a source of destruction with flooding being one of them. Flooding is an overflowing of water onto land that is normally dry. Floods can happen during heavy rains, when ocean waves come on shore, when snow melts quickly. Flooding can cause huge damage to life and property combined with various types of disease outbreaks. We have selected Problem statement 2- Increasing water-related disasters due to climate change require improved water management, resilient infrastructure, and coordinated DRR strategies to protect vulnerable communities. We will be using a combination of data analysis machine and deep learning solutions to address this problem.

What We Do

To tackle the pressing issues highlighted in our problem statement, we're developing HydroAlert, a cutting-edge AI-powered web application. This comprehensive solution is designed to revolutionize how we approach water management and safety by offering robust functionalities. HydroSense will provide advanced flood detection, leveraging AI algorithms for real-time predictions and early warnings to empower communities to prepare and respond effectively. It will also offer precise Water Quality Index measurement by analyzing various parameters, generating a clear WQI, and intelligently classifying water as either healthy or unhealthy for drinking. Beyond real-time monitoring, it will present a detailed data analysis of diverse aspects of water pollution, providing valuable insights for researchers, policymakers, and communities to understand pollution trends, identify sources, and formulate effective mitigation strategies. By integrating these features, HydroSense aims to be a vital tool for environmental monitoring, public health, and disaster preparedness, contributing to safer and healthier water resources.
